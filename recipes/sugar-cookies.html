Sugar Cookies

Description:

These sugar cookies are a simple treat that are crispy on the outside and still have a chewy center.

Ingredients

  • 2 3/4 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/4 cups margarine
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup sugar for topping

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, stir together flour, baking soda, and salt

  2. In a large bowl, cream together the margarine and 2 cups sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then the vanilla. Gradually stir in the dry ingredients until just blended.

  3. Wrap dough with plastic wrap and chill for 30-60 minutes

  4. When ready to bake, preheat the oven to 350 degress F

  5. Roll the dough into walnut-sized balls and roll the balls in the 1/4 cups of sugar. Place the cookies 2 inches apart onto ungreased cookie sheets and flatten slightly.

  6. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes until lightly browned at the edges. Allow cookies to cool on baking sheet for 5 minutes before removing to a wire rack to cool.
