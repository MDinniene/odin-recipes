Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description:

This is an easy chocolate chip cookie recipe that is great on a rainy day. Only takes about an hour to make and is plenty for a group of friends and family to share

Ingredients

  • 2 Sticks of butter
  • 3/4 cups white sugar
  • 3/4 cups brown sugar
  • 2 tsp Vanilla Extract
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 1/4 cups Flour
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Beat butter in mixer
  3. Add vanilla
  4. Add sugars and mix
  5. Add salt and baking soda
  6. Add eggs
  7. Add flour slowly, mixing after each addition
  8. Add Chocolate chips, mix only enough to distribute them
  9. Scoop onto baking sheet and cook for 9-11 minutesf
